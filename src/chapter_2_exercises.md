### Solution to exercise 2.1

Prop n.1.

$$
\sum_{x=0}^1 p(x\mid\mu) = \mu + (1-\mu) = 1  
$$

Prop n.2

$$
\mathbb E [x] = \sum_{x=0}^1 x p(x\mid \mu) =
\sum_{x=0}^1 x \cdot \mu^x (1-\mu)^{1-x} =
1 \cdot \mu^1 (1-\mu)^{1-1} = \mu
$$

Prop n.3

$$
\begin{split}
var[x] &= \sum_{x=0}^1 p(x)(x - \mu)^2 \\
&= p(0)\mu^2 + p(1)(1-\mu)^2 \\ 
&= (1-\mu)\mu^2 + \mu(1-\mu)^2 \\
&= \mu((1-\mu)\mu + (1-\mu)^2) \\
&= \mu(\mu - \mu^2 + 1 - 2\mu + \mu^2) \\
&= \mu(1-\mu)
\end{split} 
$$
